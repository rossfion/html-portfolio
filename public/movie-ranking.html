The Best Movies According to Fionn

My top 3 movies of all time

The Matrix Trilogy

In a dystopian world, humans defend themselves against the machines.

Their only hope for salvation (or, at least, some peace) rests in the one called 'Neo'.

My favorite scene is when the machines break through Zion. It starts with a trickle and then a flood

I haven't watched the 4th instalment, so I can't comment on it.

Chocolat

A nomadic mother and daughter team arrive in a very conservative French village and set up a chocolate store - during Lent.

My favourite scene has to be the "Lord of the Manor" behaving like a pig wallowing in mud.

Ghost in The Shell

A cyborg federal agent is in pursuit of "The Puppet Master" who hacks into cyborg-human minds. At some point she begins to ponder the question of her own identity.

The 2017 film was controversial as it appeared to be a case of whitewashing of Asian roles by Hollywood.

In the 1996 Japanese animation, Mira (the federal agent) is, of course, Japanese.

The song at the end of the 2017 movie by Kenji Kawai, a Bulgarian/Slavic (?) group of grannies and their daughters still sends chills up and down my spine!

I will have to see if I can find the anime. Perhaps on Crunchyroll?

As an aside, I will watch anything from Studio Ghibli :-)
